Welcome to SM Kabine General Construction

SM Kabine General Construction is a leader in providing value-added construction services to our customers by creating a successful partnership with them throughout the construction process.

Our Mission

Our mission is to use a value – for – money approach to provide quality and cost – effective civil and construction services.

Our Vision

Our aim is to create employment and skills development opportunities in communities we’re tasked to work in and to improve the standard of construction by building to expected quality using safe methods of construction.

    SM Kabine General Construction

    Building | Plumbing | Tilling | Roofing | Paving | Renovations | Electrical

    In operation since 2003, SM Kabine General Construction a 100% black owned Construction and Project Management company. We specialize in Renovations, Remodelings, New Builds, Maintenance, Material Supplies and Transport. Our services include Brickwork, Plastering, Tiling, Painting, Paving, Roofing, Plumbing and Epoxy Flooring. Our scope of services also includes project management, building plan design and material estimations. We have the experience and know-how to work on commercial and retail projects including complexes and large residential estates.
